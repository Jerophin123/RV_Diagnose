Healthy Plant

Description

The plant is healthy with no visible signs of disease. Healthy plants exhibit vigorous growth, proper leaf color, and an absence of spots, lesions, or other symptoms of disease. Maintaining healthy plants involves regular monitoring, balanced fertilization, proper irrigation, and the use of resistant varieties. Healthy plants are more resilient to environmental stresses and less likely to succumb to pests and diseases.
